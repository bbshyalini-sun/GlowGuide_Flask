Skincare Engine Knowledge Base

Welcome to the informational directory. This guide clarifies the logic models our engine executes when classifying routines based on your selection parameters.

1. Understanding Epidermal Ecosystems

Our rule validation system filters options dynamically according to four primary conditions:

  • Oily: Over-activation of lipids requiring weightless structural carriers (e.g., Gel foundations) combined with sebum-control actives such as Salicylic Acid or Niacinamide.
  • Dry: Breakdown of barrier lipids. Requires deep moisture traps like Ceramides, Humectants, and rich creams to lower trans-epidermal moisture loss.
  • Combination: Fluctuating skin parameters across regions (dry zones versus over-productive T-zones), calling for strict systemic balancing routines.
  • Sensitive: Highly reactive barrier prone to irritation. Requires calming components such as Centella Asiatica (Cica), Panthenol, and clean mineral-based UV filters.

2. Matrix Target Alignments

The core matrix runs deep calculations to target precise conditions. For example, selecting Acne flags the query to highlight formulations with anti-bacterial or micro-exfoliating properties, while selecting Redness restricts matches to soothing ingredients with anti-inflammatory characteristics.
